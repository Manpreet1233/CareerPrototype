What We Do

MENTOR | GUIDANCE | TUTORIALS | GOALS | REMINDERS | TIPS

Mentor

For clients who wish to be trained in a particular skillset can be arranged with a mentor in which they stay with their client throughout the whole course, allowing the client and mentor to build a connection on a personal level.

Guidance

Varies from mentorship as guidance is for those who wish to ask a question or two with regards to what they are learning.

Tutorials

For instance those who wish to learn a certain programming language can follow tutorials.

Reminders

As per the clients choice daily, weekly or monthly reminders are set, reminding the clients of what their goals are, keeping them on track and motivating them

Tips

Tips are given to the clients based off of the goals they have set. These tips helps individuals with slowly acheiving their goals.

Goals

The website allows one to set goals towards what they wish to achieve. Whether this be short term or long term goals. For instance if one wishes to become a computer scientist, the website laysout a whole spreadsheet showing what courses must be taken along with what extra projects they should invest their time in allowing them to use this as an outline to further set their goals.
